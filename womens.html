About the section

High performance and a place to belong.

Mentone is proud to support a strong, inclusive and high-performing women's section, competing at Premier League level while welcoming players of all ages and abilities.

We encourage new players from interstate or overseas, experienced players transferring from other clubs, and those new to hockey. With teams across Premier League, Pennant and Metro competitions, there are clear pathways for every level of ambition.

The women's program aims to balance high-performance outcomes with individual, team and club development — an environment where players are supported, challenged and connected.

Competitions

Teams for every level.

Premier League

Top-tier Hockey Victoria competition. Mentone fields Premier League women competing at the highest level in the state.

Pennant

Strong competition below Premier League with a clear pathway upward for players looking to push their game.

Metro

Competitive and social hockey for players at all stages — a great game in a welcoming environment.

Club culture

On and off the pitch.

Events are held throughout the year, including home game day barbecues, themed training sessions, preseason activities, trivia nights, cocktail events and the annual presentation night.

Whether your goal is to play at the highest level or enjoy your hockey as part of a supportive club community, Mentone has a place for you.
