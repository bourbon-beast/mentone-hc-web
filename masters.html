Section 04

Men's Masters

Men's Masters hockey at Mentone — competitive games across Hockey Victoria's Masters grades, for players 35 and over. Age brackets typically cover 35+, 45+, 55+ and 65+.

Training is Tuesday and Thursday evenings at Mentone Hockey Ground. The social side of Masters is strong — come for the hockey, stay for everything else.

Why Masters?

Still got it. Come prove it.

Hockey Victoria runs proper Masters competitions with age-grouped grades. It's competitive, well-organised and seriously fun.

The Masters community at Mentone is tight-knit and welcoming. Club rooms open after every home game — come down and see for yourself.
