### Registration

To play Hockey for our Club all players are required to be registered. Registration has two parts, firstly registration with Hockey Victoria and secondly registration with the Mentone Hockey Club. The registration process is performed online and the fees payable are dependent on the category of player 

### Transfers

If you have played for another club in Victoria or interstate, a transfer will need to be arranged from your old club. Please contact the appropriate Section Coordinator to help with this. It is better to arrange the transfer before registering with the new club. 

### Code of Conduct

Mentone Hockey Club subscribes to Hockey Victoria's Code of Behaviour and all players must agree to abide by this when registering. Our club does not condone or accept the use of bad language, rough play or abusing of either other players or umpires. Our club supports the principle of Sportsmanship and Fair Play. It is expected that players, coaches, parents and supporters recognise and encourage the contribution of all players and umpires in the correct manner. For more information on our [POLICIES ](<http://mentonehockey.org.au/administration/club-policies/>)
