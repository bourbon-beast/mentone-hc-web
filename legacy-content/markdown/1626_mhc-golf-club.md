Over the Summer months the Mentone Hockey Club runs a very active Social Golf Club. Playing once a month (roughly), between October and March , the MHC Golf Club tours some of the premier golf courses around Melbourne as well as ones that aspire to be. The MHC Golf Club is open to Men and Women to participate (and guests are also welcome). There is no joining fee and prices vary each month depending on the course and whether the option to use golf Carts is chosen. While the philosophy of the MHC Golf Club is social, the competition is intense. Each monthly round is a Stableford event, with prizes awarded on the day for the top finishers as well as longest drives and nearest the pin. The real battle is for the end of season Order of Merit Shield. This see points awarded based on the finishing order for each round completed and using the best three points tally's to determine the overall Order of Merit.
