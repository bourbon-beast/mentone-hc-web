# Injury Insurance and Member Protection Contacts

**Player Injury and Insurance Claim Process** As part of a players HV registration they are covered an Insurance Policy for Players. The coverage of this policy is limited and players are strongly advised to assess their own insurance needs **The Process to follow if injured:** Try and get the injury noted on the match report and advise a member of the Executive to act as as "Club Contact" at the first opportunity. As soon as possible download the Insurance **[Claim form](<http://hockey.jltsport.com.au/documents/Personal_Injury_Claim_Form.pdf?201103280801>) ** (Company should be informed with 30 Days of Injury) For details on coverage offered and terms and condition visit the**[JLT Sport Website](<http://hockey.jltsport.com.au/>)**   **Member Protection Information Officers** The Club currently has three, accredited Member Protection Information Officer. **What is an MPIO?** They help ensure relevant policies and procedures are clear and accessible when they are most needed by those endeavouring to resolve issues or concerns in our sport. **The role of a MPIO is to:** 1\. Be the First point of contact (usually) when unfair treatment occurs 2\. Listens to the problem 3\. Be impartial, confidential & non-directive 4\. Provide information on complaint handling options (e.g.; next step) 5\. Be a support person 6\. Be familiar with club polices & procedures   To become an MPIO, participants must be nominated to Hockey Victoria, have completed seven online modules and participated in a 3.5 hour face-to-face training program. **Who are our MPIOs?** | Name | Mobile | Email
