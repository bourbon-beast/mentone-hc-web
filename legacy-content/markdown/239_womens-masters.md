Mentone Hockey Club currently fields 2 teams in the HV Women’s Masters competition, one in +35 A grade and one in +35 B grade. The games are played on a Wednesday evening at our home ground in Keysborough (Mentone Grammar Playing Fields) and at the oppositions nominated ground. We try and carpool where we can. The A grade team is a competitive side, many of its players have represented Victoria at Women’s Masters level and regularly play in finals. In 2019 completed a “FIVEGASM” of Premiership wins. They won the flag in 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018 and again in 2019.  The ‘B’ grade section is also a strong grade and caters for players coming back to the game, taking up the game for the first time or looking for a more social experience. The 2 teams support each other when possible and we have a social dinner mid and end of the season altogether.
