# **Mentone Panthers Academy**

Mentone HC is committed to the development and support of youth athletes. The Academy will be run in Preseason, teaching skills and preparing athletes for the upcoming season. It is run by State Coach's and members of our elite teams. We have a dedicated goal keeper program at the academy.

## ****Mentone Elite Panthers****

Mentone HC has a history of producing state and national representatives. The Elite Panthers Program is about identifying athletes early and supporting their development so they have the tools to aspire to elite levels. Because of the difficulty of the program there are limited spots and athletes will be informed if they accepted.
