The Mentone Hockey Club is proud to support a strong and diverse women’s section.  Catering for all ages and skill levels, we're always eager to welcome new players from interstate or overseas, experienced players transferring from other clubs, right through to beginners. We strive to achieve a balance between the competitive side of the game along with individual, team and club development. We run plenty of events throughout the year whether this be a barbecue on home game days, themed training, preseason camps, trivia night, cocktail evenings and our annual presentation night. In 2024, the Mentone Hockey Club has entered the following teams:
Women's Premier League (1's)
Women's Premier League Reserve (2's)
Women's Pennant A (3's)
Women's Pennant B (4's)
Women's Pennant D (5's)
Women's Metro 1 (6's)
