General Information

The Mentone Hockey Club welcomes new players of all age groups and levels of experience.  We field teams in Hockey Victoria's winter, summer and indoor competitions and we cater for all age groups We are are family friendly club that has, as our vision, to  “engage, inspire, guide and develop every player to greatness.” This page seeks to provide new and prospective members all the information they need to know  .

Home Ground

Home games and training is at the Keysborough Playing Fields of Mentone Grammar, 756-758 Springvale Rd, Braeside VIC 3195

Registration

To play Hockey for our Club  all players are required to be registered.  Registration has two parts, firstly  registration with Hockey Victoria and secondly registration with the Mentone Hockey Club. The registration process is performed online and the fees payable are dependent on the category of player

Transfers

If you have played for another club in Victoria or interstate, a transfer will need to be arranged from your old club.  Please contact the appropriate Section Coordinator to help with this.  It is better to arrange the transfer before registering with the new club.

Code of Conduct

Mentone Hockey Club subscribes to Hockey Victoria's Code of Behaviour and all players must agree to abide by this when registering.   Our club does not condone or accept the use of bad language, rough play or abusing of either other players or umpires.   Our club supports the principle of Sportsmanship and Fair Play. It is expected that players, coaches, parents and supporters recognise and encourage the contribution of all players and umpires in the correct manner.   For more information on our POLICIES
