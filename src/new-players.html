New to the club? Welcome.

Whether you're a kid who's never held a stick, an adult looking for a new sport, or an experienced player moving to the area — there's a spot for you at Mentone. Here's how to start.

Find Your Starting Point

Where are you in your hockey journey?

Point us at where you're at and we'll point you at the right path into the club.

Kids & Juniors

Kids aged 5 and up. Start with Hook in2 Hockey for complete beginners, or jump straight into our junior teams if they've played before.

See the Juniors program

Adult Beginners

Never played before, or last played as a kid? No problem. We'll match you with a grade that suits your level and run you through the basics.

Talk to our Club Secretary

Experienced Players

Moving to the Bayside, or looking for a new club? We field teams from Premier League down to Metro, Saturday and midweek. Come to a training session and have a run.

Arrange a training visit
How It Works

Four simple steps from interested to on the turf.

01 · Reach out
Get in touch

Email the right person for your section — Juniors Coordinator, Men's, Women's, or Masters captain. We'll get back to you within a couple of days.

02 · Come down
Try a training session

Your first training session is free. Come along, meet some people, have a run. No pressure, no commitment.

03 · Register
Sign up online

Once you're sure, registration is a quick online process via Hockey Victoria. Pay your fees, get your uniform, you're in.

04 · Play
Start your season

We'll introduce you to your team, your coach, and your match days. Welcome to Mentone.

What You'll Need

The gear, sorted.

Don't stress about gear before you've decided to commit. For your first training or Hook in2 Hockey session, we'll lend you what you need. Once you're in, here's the kit list.

Our uniform partner stocks Mentone-branded playing gear and training kit. Sticks can be bought from any hockey retailer — Just Hockey in Moorabbin is the closest local shop.

See uniform & shop
  • Hockey stick ~$80–$400
  • Shinpads ~$30
  • Mouthguard ~$5–$80
  • Club uniform (shirt, shorts/skirt, socks) Via club shop
  • Turf shoes or runners Own
  • Water bottle Own
  • Hook in2 Hockey gear Provided
  • First training session gear Provided
