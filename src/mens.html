Our Grades

Six teams across the full pyramid.

Whatever your level and ambition, there's a Men's team at Mentone for you. New players are placed after a training session or two so we can find the right grade.

Flagship
Men's Premier League

The top of the Hockey Victoria men's pyramid. Saturday fixtures home and away, twice-weekly training, full pre-season program, and the pathway to HockeyOne and state representation.

Pre-season & selection

Pre-season starts mid-February with a combined fitness and skills block. Trial matches run through March. Team selections are announced in the final week of March, with round one typically landing the second Saturday of April.

New players — whether you're trialling for Premier League or looking for a Metro spot — are welcome at any senior training session. Just turn up; our coaches will introduce themselves.
