How to order

Order online pre-season, or buy playing uniform at the ground.

The full uniform range, including off-field items such as puffers, hoodies and other club merchandise, is available through the Belgravia Apparel online store when it opens pre-season.

When the online store is closed, players can still purchase playing uniform items from the shop at the ground. Off-field merchandise is only available through the online store when it is open.

If you are unsure what to order, email uniforms@mentonehockey.org.au or speak to your section coordinator.

Ask us
Playing numbers

Do not buy a numbered top until your number is confirmed.

Playing numbers are allocated by the club and apply to selected teams only.

Numbered playing tops are typically issued to the top men's and women's teams, including Premier League and Premier League Reserve sides, and shield-level junior teams.

If you already have a club playing number, include it when ordering. If you need a number, contact the relevant section coordinator before purchasing.

Need help?
