Common questions

Things people always ask.

Do I need to have played before?

Not at all. We have teams and programs for complete beginners through to Premier League — if you've never played, Hook in2 Hockey for kids or a Metro grade for adults is the perfect starting point.

What gear do I need?

To start, just a mouthguard and shinpads. We can lend sticks while you figure out if it's for you. Once you're registered, the club uniform is available from the club shop.

How much does it cost?

Like most community sports clubs, we run on volunteers — fees cover umpires, ground hire, equipment and Hockey Victoria levies. Registration details and fees are on our registration page.

When do I need to decide?

Happens sometimes — and that's completely fine. Come along to a training session first, meet the team and see if it feels right. No obligation to register on the spot.

I'm transferring from another club.

Welcome. Just get in touch with the relevant section coordinator and we'll guide you through the transfer process with Hockey Victoria. It's straightforward.

Can I come along before registering?

Yes. Come to training, have a hit, meet people. We'd rather you come down and get a feel for the club before committing. Just let the coordinator know you're coming.

How to join

Three steps to getting started.

01

Get in touch

Email the coordinator for your section, or just drop us a line at secretary@mentonehockey.org.au. We'll point you in the right direction.

02

Come to training

Come along, have a hit and meet the team. Training details are on the section pages. You don't need to register first — just show up.

03

Register online

When you're ready, registrations are completed online through our club portal. Takes about five minutes and you're set for the season.
