New Players

A fun, friendly first taste of hockey.

Hook in2 Hockey is Hockey Australia's national entry program for kids aged 5–10. Fun first — learning skills through games, building confidence, and making friends. No experience or gear needed to start.

  • Qualified coaches and supportive volunteers every session
  • All equipment provided — sticks, balls, shinpads
  • Small groups, lots of touches, plenty of smiles
  • A clear pathway into our Juniors teams when they're ready
