First taste of hockey

A fun, friendly first taste of hockey.

Hook in2 Hockey is Hockey Australia's national entry program for kids aged 5–10. Fun first — skills through games, building confidence and making friends. No experience or gear needed to start.

  • Qualified coaches and supportive volunteers every session
  • All equipment provided — sticks, balls, shinpads
  • Small groups, lots of touches, plenty of smiles
  • A clear pathway into our Juniors teams when they're ready

Contact: hookin2hockey@mentonehockey.org.au

Our approach

Fun first. Skills that stick.

Our junior program promotes fun, enjoyment, teamwork and a positive attitude — while helping players develop strong skills and a love of the game.

Training combines technical coaching with a small-game approach, building individual ability, teamwork and game awareness in a positive and supportive environment.

Senior players from both the men's and women's sections actively support the junior program — mentoring players and helping make the transition into senior hockey smooth and welcoming.
