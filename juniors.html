Hook in2 Hockey

Your child's first taste of hockey — fun, friendly, and no pressure.

Hook in2 Hockey is Hockey Australia's national entry program. We run it on our home ground every Thursday in term 1. It's about fun first: games, skills, and making friends. No experience, no gear, no stress.

  • All equipment provided — sticks, balls, shinpads
  • Qualified coaches and supportive volunteers
  • Small groups, lots of touches, plenty of smiles
  • Clear pathway into our U10 and U12 teams when they're ready

Match Days

Junior matches are held on Saturday mornings at Mentone Hockey Ground and at opposition grounds across Melbourne. Draws are published by Hockey Victoria at the start of each season — we send them out via our team communication channels.

Most games run between 8:00am and 12:00pm on Saturdays. Parents are always welcome to stay and watch — the club rooms are open for coffee and a sausage sandwich most weeks.

Parent FAQ

The questions we hear most.

Does my child need any experience to join?
Not at all. Hook in2 Hockey is designed for complete beginners aged 5–10. Older kids new to hockey are very welcome in our U12 and U14 squads — we run beginner-friendly sessions alongside the competitive groups.
What does my child need to bring?
For Hook in2 Hockey, just themselves, comfortable clothes, and runners. We provide sticks, balls, and shinpads. For junior competition teams, they'll need their own stick, shinpads, mouthguard, and club uniform once they're playing — we can help guide first-time parents through the shopping list.
How much does it cost?
Registration fees vary by age group and section. Hook in2 Hockey is the most affordable entry point. Junior competitive teams are priced to keep hockey accessible for Bayside families — full fee breakdowns are available on the registration page. We also accept the Victorian Government's Get Active Kids voucher.
When does the season run?
Winter season (our main competition) runs April through September, with pre-season starting in March. Summer hockey is October through February for those who want to keep playing. Juniors typically commit to winter with summer as optional.
Do I need to volunteer?
Like most community sports clubs, we run on volunteers — parents helping with team management, game-day umpiring, canteen shifts, or working bees. We're reasonable about it: even a couple of hours a season makes a real difference. Nothing is mandatory, but the club couldn't run without the families that pitch in.
What if my child wants to stop after a few weeks?
Happens sometimes — and that's completely fine. Hook in2 Hockey is low-commitment for exactly this reason. If you've registered for a full junior season and it's not working out, have a chat with the Juniors Coordinator and we'll sort something out.

Got a question about our Juniors program?

Drop our Juniors Coordinator a line — happy to help with anything from first-time questions to pathway advice.
